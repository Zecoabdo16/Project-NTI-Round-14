# 📊 HAOS - Advanced Operational Business Plan

HAOS generates predictable recurring revenue by licensing its Multi-Agent infrastructure to medical entities based on size and transaction volume, using a hybrid subscription + usage model.

| Pricing Tier | Target Segment | Pricing Model | Included Features & Token Allocation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Clinic Tier** | Polyclinics & Private Practices | **$150 / month Fixed** | Call Optimizer (Admin FAQ RAG) + Automated Chat Booking (Up to 500 interactions/mo). |

## 2. Capital Allocation & Expense Strategy (Where the Money Goes)
Operational budgets (OpEx) are balanced to optimize token costs while fueling engineering scalability and B2B market acquisition.

* **Research, Development & Engineering (40%):** Maintenance of LangGraph multi-agent systems, GraphRAG index fine-tuning, and clinical compliance safety auditing.
* **AI Infrastructure & Token Management (30%):** Covers dedicated LLM API calls, server runtime hosting on cloud infrastructures, and secure storage compliance.
* **Enterprise Marketing & B2B Client Acquisition (30%):** Funds direct sales representatives, clinical conferences, and deployment of zero-risk Proof-of-Concept (PoC) custom pilots.

## 4. Risk Assessment & Operational Safeguards
* **Patient Privacy Compliance:** Full implementation of end-to-end data encryption protocols. Data masking rules applied at the database abstraction layer to comply fully with strict healthcare standards (HIPAA/GDPR).
* **AI Clinical Mitigation:** Strict enforcement of the Gatekeeper Flow, preventing any AI agent from executing write permissions directly onto a patient's historical EHR without human verification.
